Crafted for Fun, Loved by All. Discover a punch made for everyone—formulated with 100% natural ingredients, gluten-free, and vegan-friendly.

Mike Lovin’ Punch is a pioneering punch brand, created to deliver a vibrant, refreshing, and universally inclusive beverage that enhances the vibe of any occasion.

The story of Mike Lovin’ Punch began as a nameless, homemade alcoholic creation crafted purely for “vibes.”

Initially, a simple mix of orange juice and rum was the go-to, until one classic London day—a mix of grey clouds and sudden bursts of sun—inspired a desire for something more flavorful. What starteda one-off creation became an annual staple for the Notting Hill Carnival, eagerly anticipated by friends and family alike.

Over time, the recipe was refined with thoughtful enhancements and a shift toward natural purity, evolving into a versatile, non-alcoholic punch that could be enjoyed as-is or customised with alcohol for those who preferred it. Mike Lovin’ Punch now serves as a lively, family-friendly alternative to alcoholic drinks, with the versatility to cater to both drinkers and non-drinkers.
